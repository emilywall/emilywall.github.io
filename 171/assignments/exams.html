Overview

This class has two on-paper exams: a midterm test and a final test. You will not need a personal computer for these tests -- just a pen/pencil! Mark your calendar for the exact dates of these two tests listed above.

Policies

All assessments (Quizzes/Midterm/Final Exam) must be taken on the stated date and time.

Final Exams may not be rescheduled except when a student has 3 final exams on the same calendar day. In this case, arrangements for an alternative test time must be made at least one month before final exam day.

In the event of an emergency (with proper documentation from OUE), arrangements will be handled on a case by case basis between the instructor, the student, and OUE. To be fair to all students, we can't make exceptions for individual students in non-emergency situations.
