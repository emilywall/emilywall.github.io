Extra Credit: Duet

  • Group size: Individual
  • DUE: Thursday, March 31. 11:59PM.

Overview

For 10 points of extra credit on any design sprint, you may complete the following assignment.

Duet

Duet is a product developed by the startup company Intelus.AI. You spoke earlier in the semester with the CPO, Riham Mansour. You will need to sign up for Duet for free. The system is designed to help domain experts (who may not know much about machine learning) to train machine learning models. Machine learning non-experts have very different user needs than those who have prior knowledge of machine learning. In this extra credit assignment, put yourself in the role of a user experience designer and researcher. Uou will be using Duet and giving feedback about the user experience. Read more about the system here before getting started, and check out this quick-start guide if you get stuck.

Your Task

There are three main questions you should answer:

  1. How easy is the onboarding experience and where will people be blocked?
  2. Do people understand the teaching loop the way it is offered in the platform today? Can they select the right features to fix conflicts?
  3. What applications can they build where they will need to build a classification model on Duet using any of the datasets provided whether on the platform or separately?

Deliverables

The output from this assignment should be a PDF report. In terms of length, it should be the approximate equivalent of a 5-10 minute read. Submit the PDF on Canvas. You should answer the three questions listed above. For this assignment, I would expect that to entail a minimum of the following:

  • description of how you used the system to address the question
  • critique of the usable features and areas for improvement of the system
  • justification for that critique, using the language of user-centered design and evaluation
  • accompanying annotated screenshots of the interface
In addition, please provide further critique (I like, I wish, What if) about the platform as a whole.
