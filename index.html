Emily Wall

PhD Candidate
Siemens FutureMaker Fellow, GVU Foley Scholar + more

I am a fifth year PhD candidate in Computer Science at Georgia Tech. My research interests lie in understanding users' cognitive biases based on their patterns of interaction and designing visualization interfaces to mitigate those biases.
